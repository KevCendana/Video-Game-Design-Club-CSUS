Cookie Climber

Made in the style of a game jam, this game is a quick little 'endless' scroller. You play as a kid who wants to climb to the top and get get to the cookie jar. Armed with a kick and a punch you can push and rotate blocks respectively.  

W or Space to jump, A and S to move left and right, Left arrow key for block kick, and Right arrow key for a block punch. Try your best to climb to the top!
